## Appendix

**Indicator Definitions & Calculation Notes:**
- **Bollinger Bands:** Measures price relative to volatility bands; BB Score quantifies position within bands.
- **Fibonacci Retracement:** Identifies support/resistance levels.
- **MACD-Donchian:** Combines momentum (MACD) with breakout (Donchian channel) signals.
- **Connors RSI & Z-Score:** Blends short-term RSI with statistical deviation.
- **Dual Moving Average Crossover:** EMA(50)/EMA(200) crossovers signal trend shifts; “Golden Cross” is bullish, “Death Cross” bearish.
- **Sharpe Ratio:** Risk-adjusted return metric.
- **Max Drawdown:** Largest peak-to-trough loss.
- **Return/Risk Ratio:** Total return divided by volatility.
