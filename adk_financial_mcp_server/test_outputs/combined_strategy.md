# Combined Technical and Fundamental Thesis: Apple Inc. (AAPL)

## Executive Summary

This report presents a combined technical (1-year, strategy-driven) and fundamental (3-year) analysis of Apple Inc. (AAPL), strictly grounded in MCP-provided metrics and framed for a conservative risk profile. All technical strategies currently issue HOLD or neutral signals, with no consensus for new long positions. Notably, only the Bollinger-Fibonacci and Bollinger Z-Score + RSI strategies outperformed Buy & Hold over the past year, while trend-following models underperformed. For conservative investors, maintaining existing positions with tight risk controls is prudent; new entries are not currently justified.
